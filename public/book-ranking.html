List of my top 5 books

Red Queen

Girl who lives in a world where society is divided in 2, those who have red blood and those with silver blood. Those with silver blood have superpower, and those with red blood don't, but Mare has red blood and finds out she has superpowers.

Caraval

A competition created by Legend. Donatella tries to win it in order to rescue her sister

The Darkest Mind

Dysthopian world where children suffer a disease and it gives powers. Ruby has one of the most lethal powers.

Six of Crows

Group of crazy people, the perform a crazy heist. Love Kaz.

Shatter Me

Girl with lethal touch.

Legend
